Know Us

About Company

We, ELECTROCOATING AND INSULATION TECHNOLOGIES PVT. LTD. (EITPL) an ISO 9001:2015 quality system certified company is engaged in manufacturing and selling of Epoxy Resins, Hardeners, Polyurethane and Resin formulations for more than 30 years.

M/s EITPL has UL approved Potting compounds with File No. E 255884, also potting compounds are RoHS compliance.

We have two state of the art production facilities of area 3000 m2 and 6500 m2 factory space in and around Pune, Maharashtra. We have production capacity of 20 ton/day. With such big capacity we can efficiently cater your requirements quickly.

What We Do

Epoxy, Polyurethane, Phenolic and Silicone Resins are Thermoset Polymers. We at Electrocoating & Insulation Technologies Private Limited formulate these products for use in:

  • Epoxy / Polyurethane Potting Compounds
  • Electrical Insulations
  • Electrical Insulating Powders
